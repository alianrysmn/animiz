About Us

Your gateway to exploring trends and insights in the animation film industry. Our platform is dedicated to providing in-depth analysis and data visualization to uncover the hidden patterns and preferences that drive the world of animated films.

Created as part of a research project, Animiz aims to bridge the gap between data and decision-making, empowering industry stakeholders, enthusiasts, and policymakers with meaningful insights.

At Animiz, we believe in the power of data to tell stories. Through our clean and structured datasets, intuitive visualizations, and insightful reports, we strive to help you explore the fascinating world of animation and make informed decisions that shape its future.
